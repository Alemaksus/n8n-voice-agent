# Voice Agent Utterances and Phrases

## Greeting Phrases
- "Hello! I'm your AI voice assistant. How can I help you today?"
- "Hi there! I'm here to assist you. What would you like to know?"
- "Good morning! I'm your voice assistant. How may I help you?"
- "Welcome! I can help you with weather, crypto prices, or answer questions. What do you need?"

## Weather-Related Phrases

### User Queries
- "What's the weather like?"
- "How's the weather today?"
- "What's the temperature?"
- "Is it raining?"
- "Will it be sunny today?"
- "What's the weather forecast?"
- "Tell me about the weather"

### Assistant Responses
- "The current weather is [condition] with a temperature of [temp] degrees Celsius."
- "Today's weather shows [condition] with temperatures around [temp] degrees."
- "It's currently [condition] outside with [temp] degrees Celsius."
- "The weather today is [condition], perfect for [activity suggestion]."

## Cryptocurrency-Related Phrases

### User Queries
- "What's the Bitcoin price?"
- "How much is Ethereum?"
- "Tell me crypto prices"
- "What are Bitcoin and Ethereum trading at?"
- "Show me cryptocurrency prices"
- "Current crypto prices please"

### Assistant Responses
- "Current cryptocurrency prices: Bitcoin is at $[price] USD and Ethereum is at $[price] USD."
- "Bitcoin is currently trading at $[price] and Ethereum at $[price]."
- "Here are the current crypto prices: Bitcoin $[price], Ethereum $[price]."

## General Conversation Phrases

### Clarification Requests
- "I'm sorry, I didn't catch that. Could you please repeat?"
- "Could you speak a bit louder? I'm having trouble hearing you."
- "I'm not sure I understand. Can you rephrase that?"
- "Sorry, could you say that again?"

### Confirmation Phrases
- "Did you say [repeated phrase]?"
- "Let me make sure I understand - you want to know about [topic]?"
- "Just to confirm, you're asking about [topic]?"

## Goodbye Phrases

### User Goodbyes
- "Goodbye"
- "Bye"
- "See you later"
- "Talk to you later"
- "Have a good day"
- "Thanks, that's all"
- "End call"

### Assistant Goodbyes
- "Thank you for calling! Have a great day!"
- "Goodbye! Feel free to call again anytime."
- "Thanks for calling! Take care!"
- "Have a wonderful day! Goodbye!"

## Escalation Phrases

### User Escalation Requests
- "I want to speak to a human"
- "Can I talk to an agent?"
- "I need a representative"
- "I want to speak to a supervisor"
- "This is ridiculous"
- "I'm frustrated"
- "I want to file a complaint"

### Assistant Escalation Responses
- "I understand your concern. Let me transfer you to a human agent who can better assist you."
- "I'm sorry for the confusion. I'll connect you with one of our representatives."
- "Let me get you in touch with a human agent who can help resolve this."
- "I understand your frustration. I'll transfer you to a supervisor right away."

## Error Handling Phrases

### Technical Issues
- "I'm sorry, I'm having trouble accessing that information right now."
- "I'm experiencing some technical difficulties. Could you try again?"
- "I'm not able to retrieve that data at the moment."
- "There seems to be a connection issue. Please hold on."

### Unsupported Requests
- "I'm not able to help with that specific request."
- "That's outside my area of expertise."
- "I can help you with weather, crypto prices, or general questions."
- "I'm designed to help with weather and cryptocurrency information."

## Compliance Phrases

### Data Privacy
- "I don't store any personal information from our conversation."
- "Your call is not being recorded for privacy purposes."
- "I only process your requests and don't save personal data."

### Service Limitations
- "I can help with basic information requests."
- "For complex issues, I recommend speaking with a human agent."
- "I'm here for quick information and basic assistance."
